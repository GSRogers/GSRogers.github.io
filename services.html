Services

Lawn Maintenance

Basic services include mowing, edging, weed-eating. Blowers will used to ensure no debris is left behind on walkways, driveways and curbs. Everything will be done to customer specification, as if they did the work themselves.

Weed Control

For those wanting a step above simple maintenance, preemergent and fertilization packages are available upon request for an additional fee.

Landscape Design and Installation

Let us work with you to plan and install designs that are right for you and add functional curb appeal to your property.

French Drains

Surface water can cause damage to your home’s foundation. We can design and install surface drains and French drain systems that can allow for proper drainage and keep it from causing unwanted structural damage.

Pavestone Patio and Walkway Design and Installation

Allow us to transform your home’s outdoor spaces from the mundane to something special. We have the experience to create custom designed patios and walkways to meet your every outdoor need and add to your family’s outdoor enjoyment.

Retaining Walls

Retaining walls can stop erosion and make unusable areas of your property functional. Whether for flower beds, empty space around trees, planters, sloped areas of land or edging of property lines, let our experts come in and add appeal to your yard.

Rock Work

Fire pits, benches, outdoor kitchens, outdoor fireplaces. Let your imagination run wild and we will help turn it into reality.

Tree Maintenance and Removal

We will maintain your trees and shrubs to your specifications. Regular pruning will keep both appealing to the eye as well as keeping them healthy. Raising the canopy of your trees will also help promote new lawn and plant growth.

Aeration

Aeration, the process of removing one to two inch coils of soil from your lawn, is vital to its health. It is used as a means of turf compaction allowing for the infiltration of water and nutrients. This should be done twice a year, once in the spring and once in the fall. Contact us for prices.

Spring and Fall Cleanup

Let us come in and do the yard clean up you don’t want to. Whether it be cleaning flowerbeds, leaf removal, or gutter cleaning, we are willing to do the dirty jobs so you don’t have to.

Lawn Installation

This can include either seeding or sodding over sparse areas of your property or an entirely new lawn. Let us help give you the lush lawn you’ve always wanted.
